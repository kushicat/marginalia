About

Each batch pairs a hand-crafted editorial passage with vocabulary cards and exam-style practice questions. Every vocabulary word inside a passage is highlighted — click it to jump straight to its definition card, example sentence, synonyms, and antonyms.

The questions are interactive: click an answer to reveal whether it is correct and why. Nothing is submitted or recorded anywhere.
