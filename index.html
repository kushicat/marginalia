About

Each batch pairs a hand crafted editorial/story passage with vocabulary cards and exam-style practice questions. Every vocabulary word inside a passage is highlighted, click it to jump straight to its definition card, example sentence, synonyms, and antonyms.

The questions are interactive: click an answer to reveal whether it is correct and why. Nothing is submitted or recorded anywhere.

I picked the word list from Word Smart 1. Feel Free to let me know your thoughts or suggestions. ಠ_ಠ
