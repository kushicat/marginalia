About

Each batch in Marginalia pairs a hand-crafted editorial passage with vocabulary cards and exam-style practice questions. Every word inside a passage is clickable — tap it to see the meaning, an example sentence, synonyms, and antonyms.

Generate new batches with your master prompt, paste the JSON into Add New Batch, and the batch appears instantly with all features wired up.

Words that appear constantly in exam reading sections. Master these first.

Low Frequency
Rare and specialised. Sharpens precision for top-tier verbal sections.
